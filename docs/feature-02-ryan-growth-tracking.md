# Feature 2: Ryan Growth Tracking

## Scope

Add a dedicated growth history experience for Ryan with charted height and weight trends, summary metrics, and simple unusual-pattern detection.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Ryan has a growth history with multiple height and weight measurements over time.
- The backend exposes a growth-tracking endpoint for Ryan.
- The UI displays separate height and weight visualizations.
- The UI surfaces growth insights based on the recorded measurements.
- The homepage includes a Ryan growth snapshot.

## Out of Scope

- Medical diagnosis
- Percentile benchmarking against pediatric datasets
- Clinician-facing exports
